Planets

Welcome to a place to learn about the planets of our solar system!

Our solar system consists of eight planets, a sun, dwarf planets, moons and others. The planets orbit the sun and are listed, in order of distance from the sun, Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Pluto was also included as a planet in our solar system when I was a boy, but was recently classified as a "dwarf-planet" by the International Astronomical Union in 2006.
